ABOUT US

At the forefront of interplanetary exploration, the Mars Rover Team is a group of passionate scientists, engineers, and space enthusiasts dedicated to unraveling the enigmatic mysteries of the Red Planet. With an unwavering commitment to pushing the boundaries of human knowledge, we have embarked on a remarkable journey to explore, study, and understand Mars like never before.

Our team operates at the intersection of cutting-edge technology and scientific discovery, developing and managing state-of-the-art rovers that traverse the Martian terrain, capturing breathtaking images, analyzing geological compositions, and probing the atmosphere. Armed with a collective spirit of curiosity, innovation, and perseverance, we are driven by the ambition to answer fundamental questions about the potential for past or present life on Mars, its geology, climate history, and the prospects for human exploration.

OUR ROVER

Sensors

The rover is equipped with a variety of instruments to sense its enviroment. Two cameras ensure the operator's awareness.

Expandable Modules

Different tasks require different tools. Our rover is build to quickly change its capabilities with removable modules.

Versatile manipulator

The manipulator is equipped with six rotational degrees of freedom. Which allows it to pick up objects up to 2 kg in weight.

Undercarriage

The undercarriage has a rocker suspension that can overcome difficult terrain. Each wheel can turn individually, providing advantage over differential skid steering.
